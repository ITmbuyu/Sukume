  3. Nation/Place Branding Expertise

    Nation Branding (reputation management) is one of our biggest Unique Selling Propositions (USPs). As a field, it is relatively new, highly nuanced and therefore requires high levels and hand-on experience to help nations/regions/city-branding entities to traverse the complex world of managing the perceptions of global investors, traders, tourists, event owners, etc. We have in-depth experience positioning and managing the global reputations of African nation brands. We create value propositions that depict African countries as attractive destinations for doing business, visiting, living, etc.

  4. Africa & Emerging Markets Knowledge

    We understand Africa and emerging markets. We have worked across Africa’s diverse markets from the least developed nations like Ethiopia and Lesotho to the middle to higher end economies of South Africa and Botswana. We have had to deal with the distinctions and contradictions between African cultures. We have successfully navigated the relatively unknown consumer markets of the “forgotten economies” at the bottom of the pyramid in Africa and to a lesser extent, the Middle East and Latin America.
