About Us

In Africa and other emerging markets, economies and businesses can grow and prosper while the vast majority of communities from which they derive their wealth remain poor. Besides being mind-bogglingly unjust, this creates high levels of inequality which, in turn, breeds its own social evils like crime, classism, ill-health, degradation, etc. Just like the old African philosophy "umntu ngumntu ngabantu" (roughly â€œa person derives their humanity from the value of other people); we believe that economies and businesses are what they are largely because of the communities in which they operate and that support them. As such, we are passionate about developing globally competitive brands, businesses and economies whose growth is inclusive and prosperity fairly shared with the communities involved. This is what we mean by Transformative Economic Development. We combine over 15 years experience in the private, public and non-profit sectors across the African continent.
